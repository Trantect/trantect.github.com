Trantect (Chinese: 川陀大匠) is a software development startup in Nanjing, China. Inspired by FSM and the great computer scientists, engineers and hackers of the last few decades, Trantect is looking forward to building a software development team of leading technologies, agile processes, and meaningful styles. Our talent architects and engineers are ready to work with both Chinese and international customers and partners. We have great background of computer science and technologies, including Linux kernel development, machine learning and algorithms. And we are also eager to the cutting-edge technologies, including mobile development (Android, iOS and mobile web) and high-performance server development with Node.js and Python. Our team works following customized agile and SCRUM processes and keeps optimizing them. And our architecture and domain expertise help a lot on customers’ and partners’ businesses.
